This is a browser demo for the ASCII Printer library.

Open your browser console to try the commands below.

Try it

AsciiPrinter.printByName("hello");
AsciiPrinter.printRandom("animal");
AsciiPrinter.printBySearch("rocket");
AsciiPrinter.getByName("pinkPanther");
AsciiPrinter.list("banner");
